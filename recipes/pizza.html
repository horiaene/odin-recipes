Pizza Recipe

Description

Learn to make a simple pizza

It's not that hard!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Oilive Oil
  • Salt
  • Mozarella
  • Salami
  • Tomato Sauce

Follow these steps:

  1. Knead the dough
  2. Let it rise for at least 2h
  3. Preheat the oven at 200degrees celsius
  4. Divide the dough in 6 equal parts
  5. Take one part and roll it out in an even circle
  6. Add tomato sauce
  7. Add mozzarella bits
  8. Add salami
  9. Shove in oven
  10. Bake
  11. Shove in mouth once cooled!
