Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Description

Learn to make the best pasta ever

It's not that hard!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Oilive Oil
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan
  • Guanciale
  • Egg yolks

Follow these steps:

  1. boil the pasta
  2. fry the guanciale in olive oil
  3. whisk the egg yolks and parmesan in a bowl
  4. when the pasta finishes boiling, add to guanciale pan
  5. let cool for a minute and add the egg parmesan sauce
  6. add pepper and more parmesan
  7. serve!
